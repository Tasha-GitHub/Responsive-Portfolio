About Me

Hi, I’m Tasha. I am a currently a student at the Coding Boot Camp at UT Austin. I have many hobbies which include but are not limited to the following: Skating, Hiking, Fishing and Swimming. I also foster kittens every now and then. I am very excited to learn more about the Web Development world. Please feel free to check out my profile with my current work.


When it comes to coding, I'd have to say my favorite language thus far is JavaScript. I really like manipulating components of a webpage and figuring out new and interesting tricks. I also really enjoying using JQuery.


Before I started web development, I studied Bioengineering, at Binghamton University. Shortly after that I got a position as a Clinical Engineering Manager and floated around the US sitting at hospitals that didn’t have any managers. I found this was not the best fit for me so I settled in Austin and became a Data Analyst for a Digital Advertising Company. I found that in this field, all too often I would work with AdWords Scripts. This got me interested in JavaScript and thus started this new path.
